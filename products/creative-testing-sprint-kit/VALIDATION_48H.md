# 48-Hour Validation Plan — Creative Testing Sprint Kit

## Goal
Get first 3 paid buyers or kill/reposition quickly.

## Channels (48 hours)
1. X/Twitter founders + media buyer audience (2 value posts + 1 CTA thread)
2. LinkedIn post from founder profile + 20 direct outreach DMs to agency operators
3. Existing warm contacts in performance marketing WhatsApp/Telegram groups (manual invite)

## Offer test mechanics
- 24-hour intro price: $99
- DM keyword: “SPRINT” for purchase link/manual invoice
- Deliver instant access to files after payment proof

## Success criteria
- >= 20 qualified clicks/DMs
- >= 3 purchases in 48h OR >= 10 strong buyer conversations with clear intent

## Kill criteria
- < 10 qualified responses after 2 channel pushes
- 0 purchases + weak problem resonance (“already solved with internal sheet”)

## If kill triggered
Reposition to agency white-label version with team workflow angle and retest with revised messaging.
