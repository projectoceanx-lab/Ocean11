# Claim Risk Review Checklist

## High-risk claim categories
- [ ] Guaranteed outcomes (“will erase debt”, “approved instantly”)
- [ ] Time-bound promises without substantiation
- [ ] Implied endorsements/testimonials without proof
- [ ] Medical/legal/financial advice framing

## Required controls
- [ ] Clear disclaimer placement above fold or near CTA
- [ ] Qualification language (results vary, eligibility applies)
- [ ] No deceptive urgency/countdown mechanics
- [ ] Contact/business identity visible
